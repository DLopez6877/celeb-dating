Daenerys Targaryen

Daenerys Targaryenis the younger sister of Rhaegar Targaryen and Viserys Targaryen. She's trying to cross the Narrow Sea to claim the Iron Throne and be named Queen. Maybe one day you'll be her king.

Khal Drogo

Drogo is a khal or warlord of the Dothraki. The Dothraki are fierce warriors, skilled in battle, unrelenting in combat and known for savagery towards non-Dothraki. He is guarded by bloodriders who never leave his side but he might make an exception for you.

Drogon

Drogon is one of the dragons born in the Dothraki Sea, along with Viserion and Rhaegal. He is named after Daenerys' late husband Khal Drogo. He is distinguishable by his black and red colored scales, and red-black wings. He will slither into bed with you.

Hodor

Hodor is a loyal companion. He will hold the door for you no matter what.

Ygritte

Ygritte was a quick-witted and courageous young spearwife. She had red hair and displayed great skill as an archer. Living north of the Wall, she was well skilled in survival and was staunchly loyal to the cause of Mance Rayder. If you tame this wild beast maybe she will be loyal to you.
